County Coalition 4 Lincoln County

Celebrating the strength of the human spirit and the willingness we have to make the world a better place

Welcome to the new and improved County Coalition 4 Lincoln County website, which was formerly know as the Lincoln County Community Justice. A primary focus of our group is to reduce substance abuse among youth and, over time, among adults by addressing the factors in Lincoln County, Idaho that increase the risk of substance abuse and promote the factors that minimize the risk of substance abuse. We believe that the only possible approach to prevention and the reduction of substance and alcohol abuse is through a collaborative effort that focuses on changing the full environment by identifying and implementing strategies that will affect community attitudes, perceptions, norms, and beliefs around drug use.

We are continually looking to develop creative ways to engage the youth of our communities. Rather than adults dictating the way information is to be conveyed from the top-down, it is our goal to listen to the needs and desires of the target audience themselves, the youth. We can work together and act as partners with the youth, schools, the courts, law enforcement, county government, faith based organizations, private businesses, service providers, and individuals, to pursue the goal of reducing crime, victimization, alcohol and substance abuse and work towards the building of a strong community.
