Restorative Conferencing for Lincoln County

Restorative Conferencing involves community-based processes that offer an inclusive way of dealing with offenders and victims of crime through facilitated meetings. They provide a forum in which offenders can take personal responsibility for their offending.

Benefits of Restorative Conferencing:

  • Give crime victims a chance to express their feelings directly to offenders, supported by family and friends
  • Let offenders hear directly from the people they've affected
  • Truly resolve conflicts
  • Empower offenders to take responsibility for their actions
  • Hold offenders accountable
  • Provide opportunities to decide how to repair harm
  • Provide an opportunity for healing of victims, offenders and their communities of care
  • Work toward reintegrating offenders back into their community
  • Break cycles of misbehavior, such as bullying, truancy and classroom disruption

*Lincoln County is in the process of establishing a Restorative Conferencing program that the community can access. For more information, send us an email and we will keep you informed of the progress.
